Welcome to OHBM 2020 (or what it could have been)!

Here is how to use this website to find live events (at fixed times) plus pre-recorded talks you can watch anytime.

Schedule
Use the Schedule to find live sessions and set your timezone.
Plenary Sessions
Plenary Sessions include Keynote Talks and the Business and Review meetings (pre-recorded talks + live Q&A), plus Awards ceremonies. Use the Livestream to watch live sessions in real time. Pre-recorded videos are available under Plenary Sessions.
Posters
Each paper has an abstract, poster PDF, and live Jitsi room for Q&A at different times, and a linked RocketChat channel (for chatting anytime with the author).
Tutorials and Workshops
The X tutorials (on Date1-Date2) and Y workshops are included (or not, we all need to eat) under conference registration. Some are fully pre-recorded, others include live sessions as well!
Special Interest Groups
Here we can reference dedicated pages for SIGs with their own whatevers.
Sponsors
Meet our (well -- ACL2020, thank you!) sponsors.
